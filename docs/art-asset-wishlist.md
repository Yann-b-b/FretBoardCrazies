# FretBoardCrazies — Art Asset Wishlist (Playful & Friendly)

A reference list of images to generate to make the app more approachable and
better-looking. Direction: **playful & friendly** (rounded, warm, a little
mascot charm — think Duolingo/Headspace, not a sterile pro tool).

## Goal
Learning the fretboard feels intimidating. The art should make the app feel
**encouraging and inviting** (you'll come back daily) and give it a **cohesive
visual identity** instead of stock SF Symbols on flat panels.

## Style guide (keep everything consistent)
- **Mood:** warm, friendly, bouncy, rewarding. Chunky rounded shapes, thick
  clean outlines, soft drop shadows, gentle gradients.
- **Palette:**
  - Primary warm orange `#FF8A3D`, amber `#FFB23E`
  - Friendly secondary teal `#2DD4BF` / green `#34C759`
  - Soft cream background `#FFF6EC`; cozy dark `#221A14` (dark mode)
  - Pops: coral `#FF6B6B`, sky `#4DA3FF`
  - Belt colors stay canonical (white→black ladder)
- **Reusable prompt prefix** (paste before each item's prompt):
  > "flat vector illustration, playful and friendly, rounded chunky shapes,
  > thick clean outlines, soft drop shadow, warm palette (orange/amber + teal),
  > transparent background, high detail, centered"
- **Don't:** photorealism, harsh gradients, thin/spindly lines, muddy colors,
  busy backgrounds behind UI text.

## Consistency: the style anchor
Cohesion comes from a single approved **anchor image** (`art/_anchor.png`), not a
character. Every asset is generated by editing the anchor, so palette, line
weight, and shading carry across the set. See `scripts/README.md`.

## Priority 1 — Identity (biggest impact)

### 1. App icon
- **Where:** Dock, Launchpad, About — the first impression.
- **Prompt:** prefix + "app icon: a cheerful stylized guitar fretboard with one
  glowing note dot, rounded square, warm orange-to-amber background, friendly,
  iconic, simple silhouette readable at small sizes"
- **Output:** 1024×1024 master PNG (no alpha for macOS icon). Also export the
  standard set: 16, 32, 128, 256, 512 @1x and @2x.

### 2. Belt badges (×8) + belt-up glow
- **Where:** Drill header chip + Progress belt card; the glow version on a
  belt-up celebration.
- **Prompt (per belt):** prefix + "a cute knotted martial-arts belt icon in
  <COLOR>, tied in a bow with two hanging ends, glossy, rounded, badge style"
  — repeat for White, Yellow, Orange, Green, Blue, Purple, Brown, Black.
- **Output:** transparent PNG, 512×512 (downscale in-app). One extra "belt-up"
  version with a starburst/glow behind it.

---

## Priority 2 — Game feel & reward

### 3. Combo flame set + milestone burst
- **Where:** the 🔥 combo badge in the Drill; milestone bursts at 5/10/20.
- **Prompt:** prefix + "a friendly cartoon flame, three sizes from a small
  spark to a big roaring flame, expressive, orange-yellow gradient" and
  separately "a celebratory burst graphic with stars and sparks, 'combo'
  energy"
- **Output:** transparent PNGs, ~256–512px; deliver flame S/M/L as a set.

### 4. Success / "Correct!" sticker
- **Where:** pops on a correct answer (extra sparkle when fast).
- **Prompt:** prefix + "a happy confetti/starburst sticker that reads energy of
  'Nice!', rounded, celebratory, small accent graphic"
- **Output:** transparent PNG, ~256px; maybe a 'fast!' variant.

---

## Priority 3 — Warmth & polish

### 6. Fretboard skin
- **Where:** layered *behind* the code-drawn fretboard (dots/lines stay drawn).
- **Prompt:** prefix-ish (slightly richer) + "a stylized warm wood guitar
  fretboard panel, smooth grain, soft metallic fret wires, pearl inlay dots at
  3/5/7/9/12, friendly not photorealistic, horizontal"
- **Output:** wide PNG (e.g. 1600×500), tileable-ish; keep it subtle so text
  stays readable.

### 7. Background gradient / texture
- **Where:** behind the screens for warmth.
- **Prompt:** prefix + "a very subtle soft warm gradient background, cream to
  pale amber, faint paper texture, non-distracting"
- **Output:** large PNG or just recreate as a SwiftUI gradient (cheapest).

---

## Priority 4 — Approachability / onboarding

### 8. Empty-state illustrations
- **Where:** Trend chart "play on more days" state; idle Drill screen.
- **Prompt:** prefix + "a friendly empty-state illustration — a relaxed guitar
  next to an empty chart, warm and encouraging" (no mascot character)
- **Output:** transparent PNG, ~600px.

### 9. "How it works" 3-step art
- **Where:** an optional first-run intro.
- **Prompt:** prefix + three scenes: "1) a note name + string shown, 2) hands
  playing a guitar, 3) a stopwatch getting faster — simple friendly icons"
- **Output:** transparent PNGs, ~512px each, matching set.

### 10. String-preset thumbnails
- **Where:** the E·A / E·A·D / … preset buttons.
- **Prompt:** prefix + "tiny fretboard icon with the lowest N strings
  highlighted in orange, the rest dimmed, minimal"
- **Output:** transparent PNG, 128px; one per preset (E·A, E·A·D, E·A·D·G,
  +B, all 6).

---

## Lower priority / skip for now
- Custom tab-bar icons (SF Symbols are fine and consistent).
- Tuner dial/needle gauge (nice, but niche).

## Production specs (so they drop in cleanly later)
- Generate **high-res** (1024px+) and downscale; keep **transparent PNG** for
  everything except the app icon.
- Provide **@1x/@2x** (macOS is mostly @2x Retina) or, ideally, **SVG/PDF
  vector** for icons/badges so they scale crisply.
- Mind **dark mode** — give belt badges and other assets a light outline or shadow so they
  read on the dark Drill panel.
- They'll live in `Assets.xcassets`; the app currently uses SF Symbols, so each
  asset replaces a symbol or fills a new spot.
